Base Game Rules

This is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot. Select your denomination and total bet, then press SPIN to play.

Line Wins
Winning symbol combinations start on the leftmost reel and pay from left to right across adjacent reels.
Scatter Wins
Scatter symbols pay in any position on the screen and also award Free Games when 3 or more land.
Wild Symbol
Wild substitutes for all regular paying symbols to help complete line wins. Wild does not substitute for Scatter or the bonus symbol.
Bonus Symbol
The 1x1 symbol triggers the Lock-In bonus. Landing 6 or more on one base or free spin starts the feature.

Free Games Trigger

3 Scatters award 5 Free Games.

4 Scatters award 10 Free Games.

5 Scatters award 15 Free Games.

During Free Games, additional Scatters can award more spins.

Bonus Features

S
Free Games
3, 4, or 5 Scatters award Free Games. During the feature, more Scatters can add extra Free Games.
1x1
Lock-In Bonus
Land 6 or more 1x1 symbols to trigger the Lock-In feature. New 1x1 symbols reset the spin counter. Filled spaces can merge into larger blocks for bigger awards.
Lock-In Awards
Every collected block awards a prize based on block size. Larger merged blocks have a chance to award Mini, Minor, Major, or Grand jackpots.
Important Notes
  • All line wins are multiplied by your selected total bet.
  • Scatter wins are paid in addition to line wins.
  • Only the highest win for each line is paid.
  • Values shown here are displayed as bet multipliers for clarity.
