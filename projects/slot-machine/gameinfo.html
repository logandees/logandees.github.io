Short n' Sweet Slots

Match symbols across 10 fixed lines, trigger free games with Scatters, and land Lucky Lock-in-Place symbols for a merging bonus feature.

PAGE 1

How To Play

Set your denomination in the bottom right, adjust your bet, and press SPIN to start the reels.

  • All wins pay from left to right on 10 fixed paylines.
  • Only the highest win on each payline is awarded.
  • Win amounts are based on your current total bet.
  • Jackpot prize amounts increase with higher denomination.
  • Jackpot prize odds increase with higher bet.
  • Wilds substitute for all regular paying symbols.
  • Scatters award free games when 3 or more appear.
Free Games are triggered by Scatter symbols, while the Lock-in-Place bonus is triggered by landing 6 or more Lucky Lock-in-Place symbols.

Feature Details

Free Games Feature
Land 3, 4, or 5 Scatter symbols to trigger 5, 10, or 15 free spins. Extra Scatters during the feature may award additional free spins.
Wild Symbol
The Wild symbol substitutes for regular pay symbols to improve your chances of completing winning combinations on paylines.
Lock-in-Place Bonus
Landing 6 or more Lucky Lock-in-Place symbols triggers the Lock-in-Place bonus. Adjacent symbols merge into larger blocks, increasing prize amounts and improving jackpot potential.
Bonus During Free Games
The Lock-in-Place bonus can also be triggered during free games, giving you a chance to stack both features together.

Quick Notes

  • The game checks all 10 fixed paylines on every paid spin.
  • Scatter symbols do not need to land on a payline to award free games.
  • The Lock-in-Place bonus is based on the number and position of Lucky Lock-in-Place symbols.
  • Use the PAYTABLE button on the main game for exact payout values.

Free Games Overview

Free Games are the main repeat-spin bonus mode in the game. During this feature, the reels spin without deducting another paid wager for each awarded free spin.

  • 3 Scatters award 5 free spins.
  • 4 Scatters award 10 free spins.
  • 5 Scatters award 15 free spins.
  • Extra Scatters during free games may award additional spins.
  • Total free game winnings are added together before the feature ends.

Lock-in-Place Overview

The Lock-in-Place bonus is triggered by landing 6 or more Lucky Lock-in-Place symbols. During the bonus, adjacent symbols can join together into larger merged shapes.

  • Larger merged shapes create bigger prize amounts.
  • Bigger merged results can lead to higher jackpot awards.
  • Symbol position matters because adjacent symbols combine together.
  • This bonus can also be won during free games.

General Rules

  • Wins are evaluated on 10 fixed paylines only.
  • Only the highest win per payline is paid.
  • Scatter symbols do not need to land on a payline to trigger free games.
  • Wild symbols do not replace Scatter symbols.
  • Your denomination and total bet affect the value of wins and jackpots.

Gameplay Tips

  • Higher denomination increases jackpot prize values.
  • Higher bet increases jackpot prize odds.
  • Watching for 3 or more Scatters is the clearest sign that free games may trigger.
  • Clusters of Lucky Lock-in-Place symbols are especially important because adjacent symbols can merge.
