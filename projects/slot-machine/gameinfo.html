How To Play

Set your denomination, adjust your bet, and press SPIN to start the reels.

  • All wins pay from left to right on active fixed paylines.
  • The game uses 10 fixed lines.
  • Only the highest win on each payline is awarded.
  • Win amounts are based on your current total bet.
  • Wilds substitute for regular paying symbols unless otherwise noted.
  • Scatters can trigger free games when enough appear.

Feature Details

Free Games Feature
Land 3 or more Scatter symbols to trigger free games. Extra Scatters during the feature may award additional spins depending on the result.
Wild Symbol
The Wild symbol substitutes for regular pay symbols to improve your chances of completing winning combinations on paylines.
Lock It In Bonus
Oversized linked symbol shapes may appear and can merge into larger blocks. These can create stronger line coverage and special feature outcomes.
Jackpot-Style Awards
Certain Lock It In outcomes can award Mini, Minor, Major, or Grand-style prizes depending on the completed block and result tier.

Quick Notes

  • The game uses fixed paylines, so each paid spin checks the full line set.
  • Scatter awards do not need to complete a normal payline pattern.
  • Feature results vary depending on symbol placement and final reel outcomes.
  • Use the PAYTABLE button on the main game for exact payout values.

Free Games Overview

The Free Games feature is the main repeat-spin bonus mode in the game. During this feature, the reels spin without deducting another paid wager for each awarded free spin.

  • Triggered by landing 3 or more Scatters.
  • Extra Scatters during the feature may award more free games.
  • Free Games can create strong combo potential across the 10 fixed lines.
  • Total feature winnings are added together before the feature ends.

Lock It In Overview

Lock It In is tied to linked symbol activity and oversized shape behavior. When special linked layouts appear, symbols can combine into larger forms that cover more reel positions and create stronger outcomes.

  • Linked shapes can expand into larger blocks.
  • Larger blocks can improve payline coverage.
  • Some results may lead to bonus-style prize awards.
  • Final feature results depend on the completed symbol pattern.

General Rules

  • Wins are evaluated on 10 fixed paylines only.
  • Only the highest win per payline is paid.
  • Scatter awards do not need to land on a payline to trigger features.
  • Wild substitutes do not replace Scatter symbols.
  • The current denomination and total bet affect the value of wins.
