Short n' Sweet Slots

Match sweet symbols across 20 fixed lines, trigger free games with Scatters, and land oversized Lock It In symbols for bigger line wins and jackpot-style bonus moments.

How To Play

Set your denomination, adjust your bet, and press SPIN to start the reels.

  • All wins pay from left to right on active fixed paylines.
  • Only the highest win per payline is paid.
  • Wins are multiplied by your current total bet denomination value.
  • Wilds substitute for all regular paying symbols unless otherwise noted.
Free Games: 3 or more Scatter symbols award free spins.
Lock It In: Large linked symbols can create oversized winning blocks and special bonus payouts.

Feature Details

Free Games Feature Land 3 or more Scatter symbols to trigger free games. Additional Scatters during the feature may award more free spins depending on your reel result configuration.
Wild Symbol The Wild symbol substitutes for regular pay symbols to improve your chances of completing winning combinations on paylines.
Lock It In Bonus Oversized linked symbol shapes may appear and can merge into larger blocks. These can create stronger line coverage and also feed special bonus-style payouts.
Jackpot-Style Awards Certain Lock It In outcomes can award Mini, Minor, Major, or Grand-style prizes based on the achieved block and result tier.

Feature Pays & Notes

Scatter Pays / Free Games Trigger 3 or more Scatter symbols can trigger free games. Scatter behavior is evaluated independently of standard line wins.
Lock It In Large Symbols Linked shapes can cover multiple reel positions. These may count across several paylines at once depending on the final merged shape.
Mini / Minor / Major / Grand Special Lock It In outcomes may award jackpot-style prizes, scaled to the current denomination level.
Only Highest Line Win Per Line If more than one win could occur on the same payline, only the highest win is awarded for that line.
